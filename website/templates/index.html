About

Hotel California is a 4-star hotel located at the heart of Dublin City. It's just minutes away from the Guinness Storehouse and the National Museum of Ireland Decorative Arts & History. Hotel California offers a warm and welcoming home-from-home atmosphere.
We have 50 en-suite rooms fitted with modern amenities such as a free high-speed internet. We also have a restuarant with some nation-renowned chefs to provide you some fine-dining experience.




Services

We provide a wide range of services from room-service, gym, dry-cleaning, spa, swimming pool (with pool side bar) for all our valued guest.
